About Me

I’m Loraine Condicion, 18 years old, from Palincaro, Tuy, Batangas. I’m a first-year Information Technology student at Batangas State University, quietly building my dreams one line of code at a time. I’m an introvert by nature, but when I feel safe and connected — especially with close friends — I bloom into someone vibrant and expressive. I’m emotional, loving, cheerful, and deeply faithful. I entrust everything I do to God, always seeking His guidance before I take a step. I overthink, I worry, and I carry silent pressures — but I smile through them. I’m simple, not the smartest, not the best at singing or dancing, but I still sing and dance anyway. I love listening to music, and fries are my comfort food. I don’t want to live a life trapped in hardship. I dream of rising, of becoming more. I am like the rain — soft, steady, sometimes overlooked, but always present. This is me: imperfect, emotional, faithful, and full of quiet wonder.
