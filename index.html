About Me

I’m Loraine Condicion, 18 years old, from Palincaro, Tuy, Batangas. I’m a first-year Information Technology student at Batangas State University, quietly building my dreams one line of code at a time. I’m an introvert by nature, but when I feel safe and connected — especially with close friends — I bloom into someone vibrant and expressive. I’m emotional, loving, cheerful, and deeply faithful. I entrust everything I do to God, always seeking His guidance before I take a step. I overthink, I worry, and I carry silent pressures — but I smile through them. I’m simple, not the smartest, not the best at singing or dancing, but I still sing and dance anyway. I love listening to music, and fries are my comfort food. I don’t want to lve a life trapped in hardship. I dream of rising, of becoming more. I am like the rain — soft, steady, sometimes overlooked, but always present. This is me: imperfect, emotional, faithful, and full of quiet wonder.

Me In BGC/VGC

This place is the Venice Grand Canal Mall in BGC, and I consider it one of the most beautiful and memorable places I’ve ever visited. I’ve been there three times, and each visit holds a special memory. The first time, I went with my older sister; the second, we were joined by our sibling; and the last time, it was just me, my sister, and our mom. It was during Christmas—we watched a movie and had dinner at a restaurant there. That day became our final bonding moment with our mom before she passed away due to cancer. Although it’s painful, I find comfort in knowing that she is now healed and at peace in the presence of the Lord. This place will always hold a special place in my heart, not just for its beauty, but for the precious memories it gave me.
